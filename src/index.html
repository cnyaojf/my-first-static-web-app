Do you want to be heard, seen and understood? I am here listening. In my extensive 19+ years of clinical experience, I've come to appreciate the profound impact of genuine listening, a practice often scarce in the world. If we, as a society, could emulate the empathetic listening we provide as psychotherapists, we might alleviate much of the pain and suffering. In the realm of clinical work, listening becomes a powerful tool. When clients feel heard, seen, under and accepted, a significant portion of the healing process unfolds.
In my clinical practice, empathetic listening fosters a deep understanding of my clients' narratives, alleviating feelings of inadequacy. This connection cultivates self-awareness and self-compassion, empowering them to make informed decisions for their future.
My approach typically begins with a comprehensive assessment to understand each client's story and the underlying reasons for their experiences. We collaborate to reach a shared understanding of their situation and identify areas for growth. I tailor treatments to meet each client's specific needs and circumstances.
